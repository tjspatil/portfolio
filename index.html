Where Automotive Meets Software Engineering

I'm a Software Engineer based in Dublin, Ohio, with a Master's in Computer Science (GPA: 3.70) from the University of Dayton and 3+ years of hands-on experience developing diagnostic and manufacturing software for the automotive industry. My day-to-day involves writing Java-based systems that communicate with vehicle ECUs using protocols like UDS, J1939, and CAN.

From End-of-Line testing on assembly floors to cloud infrastructure powering SaaS platforms, I bridge the gap between embedded vehicle systems and modern software practices. I'm also a 2x published researcher with work in UAV swarm systems and IoT devices.
